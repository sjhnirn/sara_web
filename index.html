About

Hi, I'm Sara Hosseini.

I am a freelance photographer with a passion for finding beauty in the everyday. My work spans striking portraiture, mouth-watering food, and refined product photography. I believe in a cinematic approach to visual storytelling.

With over 7 years of experience, I've developed a signature style that focuses on rich color, dynamic composition, and evoking real emotion — whether I'm working in a quiet studio or on location in Tehran.
